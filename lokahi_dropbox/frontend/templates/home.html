Lokahi Fintech

What we do

Lokahi Fintech Crowdfunding (LFC) brings together small investors and small firms needing venture capital which would otherwise be excluded from this financial market. Companies seeking venture capital need a means to disseminate information about their company and its potential growth and profit in a safe and secure environment. Potential investors need a means to collect, share, and distribute information about the potential investments in a similar environment. The goal of this website is to develop a secure system in which companies and investors can work together to share feedback and avoid asymmetric information and moral hazards.
